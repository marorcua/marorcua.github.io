Home

A MERN (Mongodb, Expressjs, Reactjs, Nodejs) ecommerce app made from scratch as my third project at Ironhack

Home is an e-commerce platform of high-cost furniture, in which there are 3 complete users with their respective views (admin, supplier and client) and works very similar to Amazon.

  • Clients can see products, include them in their wishlist and acquire them
  • Suppliers create the products and determince different options per product
  • Administrator has a dashboard that includes main kpis, graphs and a list of clients and suppliers
  • Other technologies implemented: stripe, nodemailer, nivo
